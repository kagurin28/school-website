About New Town High

There are many amazing aspects of New Town High.


Eshays

Eshays (Eshayus Dominicus) are one of the many creatures found at New Town High. They bear atop their heads what they call a "mullet". It is thought that these "mullets" are to show the social status of themselves, the longer the mullet, the higher the status.
